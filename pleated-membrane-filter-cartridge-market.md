PES (Polyethersulfone) filters provide low protein binding and high flow rates, suitable for biopharmaceutical applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filters excel in micro-filtration of aggressive chemicals and solvents. Nylon filters are versatile, known for their strength and compatibility with various liquids, often used in laboratories and research settings.</p></p>
