<p><p>The inventory software market comprises two primary types: cloud-based inventory software and on-premise inventory software. Cloud-based inventory software is hosted on remote servers, offering users scalable, accessible solutions that enhance real-time collaboration and reduce upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise inventory software is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different business needs, depending on size, budget, and operational preferences.</p></p>
