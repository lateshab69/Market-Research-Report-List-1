<p><p>UV-curable flexographic ink is a type of ink used in the flexographic printing process that cures or dries when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. This technology allows for faster drying times and offers superior adhesion and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for packaging, labels, and other high-quality printing applications. The advantages of UV-curable inks include their eco-friendliness, as they emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs) compared to traditional solvent-based inks.</p><p>The UV-curable flexographic ink market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
