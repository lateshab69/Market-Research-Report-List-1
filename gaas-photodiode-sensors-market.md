<p><p>The GaAs photodiode sensors market encompasses several types, primarily categorized into PIN, avalanche, and Schottky photodiodes. PIN GaAs photodiodes feature a p-type, intrinsic, and n-type structure, offering high sensitivity and speed, ideal for communication applications. Avalanche GaAs photodiodes provide internal gain through avalanche multiplication, enhancing performance in low-light scenarios. Schottky GaAs photodiodes utilize a metal-semiconductor junction, enabling rapid response times. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialized designs and configurations catering to niche applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
