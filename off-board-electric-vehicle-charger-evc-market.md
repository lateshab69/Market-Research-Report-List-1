<p><p>The Off-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC) market includes Level 2 and Level 3 chargers. Level 2 chargers operate at 240V, providing faster charging than standard outlets, ideal for homes, workplaces, and public stations. They typically deliver 10-60 miles of range per hour. Level 3 chargers, or DC fast chargers, use high-voltage direct current to charge EVs rapidly, offering 60-100 miles of range in 20 minutes. They are suited for commercial use, highways, and fleet operations, enabling quick turnaround for long-distance travel. Both cater to growing EV adoption and infrastructure needs.</p></p>
