<p><strong>The Bonded Seal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non Self Centralising Type</li><li>Self Centralising Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bonded seal market is categorized into two main types: non-self centralizing and self-centralizing seals. Non-self centralizing seals require manual alignment during installation, making them suitable for applications where precision is less critical. In contrast, self-centralizing seals automatically align themselves upon installation, ensuring a perfect fit and enhanced sealing performance, which is ideal for high-pressure environments. Both types serve distinct applications across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, catering to different sealing needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
