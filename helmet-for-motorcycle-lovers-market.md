Full face helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head, including the chin and face, making them ideal for high-speed riders. Open face helmets offer facial exposure while protecting the top, providing a balance of safety and comfort, popular for cruising. Half helmets, or beanies, provide minimal coverage, prioritizing freedom and style, often favored by urban riders seeking a lightweight option.</p></p>
