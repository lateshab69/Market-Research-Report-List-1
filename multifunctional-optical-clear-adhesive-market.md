<p><p>The multifunctional optical clear adhesive market includes resistive optical clear adhesives and capacitive optical clear adhesives. Resistive optical clear adhesives are primarily used for touchscreens that rely on pressure to detect input, offering excellent optical clarity and bond strength. In contrast, capacitive optical clear adhesives are designed for touchscreens that sense electrical changes, providing a smooth, responsive touch experience. Both types cater to the increasing demand for high-performance displays in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, emphasizing durability and visual quality.</p></p>
