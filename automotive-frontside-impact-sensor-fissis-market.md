<p><p>The Automotive Front Impact Sensor (FIS) detects collisions at the front of a vehicle, triggering safety systems like airbags to protect occupants. It is typically mounted in the bumper or front frame. The Side Impact Sensor (SIS) identifies side collisions, activating side airbags or curtain airbags for enhanced safety. Both sensors are critical for modern vehicle safety systems, with FIS focusing on frontal impacts and SIS addressing side impacts, ensuring comprehensive protection in various crash scenarios.</p></p>
