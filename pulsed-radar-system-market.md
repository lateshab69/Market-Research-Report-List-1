<p><strong>The Pulsed Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L Band</li><li>X Band</li><li>C Band</li><li>S Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulsed radar system market is categorized primarily into four frequency bands: L Band, S Band, C Band, and X Band. L Band (1-2 GHz) is known for long-range detection, making it suitable for air traffic control and weather monitoring. S Band (2-4 GHz) is used for aircraft surveillance and maritime applications. C Band (4-8 GHz) offers a balance of range and resolution, often employed in military and civilian radar systems. X Band (8-12 GHz) provides higher resolution, commonly utilized in precision tracking and imaging applications.</p></p>
