<p><p>The PV glass market comprises various types tailored for photovoltaic applications. Tempered glass is strengthened through heating, offering durability and safety, which is crucial in solar installations. TCO (Transparent Conductive Oxide) glass provides electrical conductivity while maintaining transparency, essential for optimal solar cell performance. Other market types may include specialized coatings or materials designed to enhance efficiency, protect against environmental factors, or reduce weight in solar panel designs.</p></p>
