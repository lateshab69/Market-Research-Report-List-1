<p><p>The fruit hardness tester market comprises two main types: digital fruit hardness testers and meter pointer hardness testers. Digital fruit hardness testers use electronic sensors to provide precise measurements, often displaying results on a screen, which enhances ease of use and accuracy. In contrast, meter pointer hardness testers rely on analog mechanisms, where a needle points to a scale, offering a straightforward, mechanical approach. Both types serve the agricultural industry, helping assess fruit ripeness and quality for optimal harvesting and marketing.</p></p>
