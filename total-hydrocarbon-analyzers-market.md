<p><p>The Total Hydrocarbon Analyzers market comprises several types: Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) which measure hydrocarbon concentration based on ionization by combustion; Gas Chromatographs (GCs) that separate and analyze compounds in gas samples; Catalytic Sensors that detect hydrocarbons through catalytic oxidation; Photo-Ionization Detectors (PID) that utilize ultraviolet light to ionize compounds for detection; and Infra-Red (IR) Sensors that measure hydrocarbon levels via absorption of IR light. Each technology serves specific environmental monitoring and industrial applications.</p></p>
