<p><p>Processing aids for PVC film are additives that enhance the processing characteristics and performance of polyvinyl chloride. They are available in two primary forms: prill and powder. Prill refers to small, round pellets that facilitate easier handling and mixing during production. Powder, on the other hand, is fine and can provide superior dispersion and blending properties in formulations. Both types are crucial in optimizing the processing efficiency, flexibility, and durability of PVC films in various applications.</p></p>
