<p><p>Radar Water Level Meters are categorized into two primary types based on frequency: Low Frequency Radar and High Frequency Radar. Low Frequency Radar operates at frequencies below 1 GHz, making it suitable for measuring large bodies of water, providing accurate readings in turbulent conditions. In contrast, High Frequency Radar, typically above 1 GHz, excels in detecting precise water levels in smaller, more challenging environments like rivers or tanks. Both types enhance efficiency in water management across various industries.</p></p>
