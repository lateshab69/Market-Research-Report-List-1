<p><p>The Towed Array Sonar System market is segmented into passive and active sonar types. Passive sonar relies on listening for sounds emitted by vessels or marine life, making it ideal for surveillance and stealth operations. It excels in detecting submarines without revealing the listener's position. In contrast, active sonar emits sound pulses and analyzes their echoes to determine the distance and speed of objects, providing detailed underwater mapping and target tracking. Both systems play crucial roles in naval defense and maritime security.</p></p>
