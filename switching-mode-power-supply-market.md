<p><p>The Switching Mode Power Supply (SMPS) market comprises two primary types: AC/DC and DC/DC converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current from mains power into direct current for various electronic devices. They are widely used in applications like computers and chargers. Conversely, DC/DC converters adjust the voltage levels within DC power circuits, enabling devices to operate efficiently with variable voltage demands. Both types support a range of applications, from consumer electronics to industrial systems, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing power losses.</p></p>
