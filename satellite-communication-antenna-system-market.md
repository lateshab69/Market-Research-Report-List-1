<p><p>The satellite communication antenna system market comprises various types, notably the Ka-band and Ku-band antenna systems. Ka-band antennas operate in the 26.5 to 40 GHz frequency range, offering higher data rates and bandwidth, suitable for advanced applications like high-definition broadcasting and broadband services. In contrast, Ku-band antennas, functioning between 12 and 18 GHz, are widely used for television broadcasting, satellite communication, and internet services due to their reliable performance and cost-effectiveness in various conditions, including adverse weather.</p></p>
