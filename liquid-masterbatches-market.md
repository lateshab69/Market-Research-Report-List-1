<p><p>The liquid masterbatches market comprises three main types: black masterbatches, white masterbatches, and color masterbatches. Black masterbatches incorporate carbon black for enhanced UV protection and opacity, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. White masterbatches use titanium dioxide to achieve brightness and opacity, commonly utilized in packaging and film. Color masterbatches contain concentrated pigments for varying hues, providing flexibility in design and aesthetics. Together, these masterbatches serve diverse industries, enhancing product performance and visual appeal.</p></p>
