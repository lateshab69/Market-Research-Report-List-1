<p><p>The Flexo inks market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize volatile organic compounds that provide excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various substrates like plastic and metal. In contrast, water-based inks are eco-friendlier, using water as a primary solvent, which reduces harmful emissions and improves safety during application. Both types are essential in flexographic printing, serving diverse industries such as packaging, labeling, and textiles, while catering to varying environmental regulations and performance needs.</p></p>
