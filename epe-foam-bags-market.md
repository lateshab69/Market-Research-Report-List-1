<p><p>EPE foam bags are categorized based on density levels of polyethylene used in their production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) bags are strong and durable, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) offers a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for various packaging needs. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) bags are lightweight and flexible, commonly used for shipping and protective wrapping. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines flexibility with tensile strength, making it versatile for both industrial and consumer markets.</p></p>
