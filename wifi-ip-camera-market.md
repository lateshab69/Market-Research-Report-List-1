<p><p>The WiFi IP camera market is divided into centralized and decentralized types, each catering to different surveillance needs. Centralized IP cameras transmit video data to a central server for processing and storage, enhancing management and control, making them suitable for large-scale installations. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras process and store data locally, often on the device itself or edge storage solutions, allowing for increased flexibility and reduced reliance on network infrastructure, ideal for smaller or remote applications.</p></p>
