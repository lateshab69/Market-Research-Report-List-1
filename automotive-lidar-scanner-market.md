<p><strong>The Automotive LiDAR Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive LiDAR Scanner market is categorized into Mechanical LiDAR and Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to emit laser beams and capture 3D environmental data, offering high precision but with bulkier designs and higher costs. Solid-State LiDAR, on the other hand, relies on fixed components without moving parts, providing compact, durable, and cost-effective solutions. While Mechanical LiDAR is widely used in advanced applications, Solid-State LiDAR is gaining traction due to its scalability and suitability for mass-market automotive integration.</p></p>
