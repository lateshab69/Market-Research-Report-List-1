<p><strong>The Mobile MRI Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field Strength</li><li>Mid Field Strength</li><li>High Field Strength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile MRI scanner market is categorized into three types based on field strength: low, mid, and high field strength. Low field strength MRI scanners (typically below 1.0 Tesla) are portable, cost-effective, and suitable for basic imaging needs, but provide lower resolution. Mid field strength scanners (1.0 to 1.5 Tesla) offer a balance of image quality and mobility, making them versatile for various clinical applications. High field strength scanners (above 1.5 Tesla) deliver superior image quality and detail, catering to specialized medical diagnostics, though they are less portable.</p></p>
