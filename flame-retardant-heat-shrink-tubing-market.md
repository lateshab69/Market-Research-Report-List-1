<p><p>Flame retardant heat shrink tubing is used for insulation, protection, and sealing in electrical and industrial applications. **Single Wall Tubing** is a basic type, offering heat resistance, insulation, and flame retardancy in a single layer. **Dual Wall Tubing** features an additional adhesive inner layer, providing enhanced sealing, moisture resistance, and mechanical protection.
