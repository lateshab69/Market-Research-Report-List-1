<p><p>The flight simulator market consists of several types, including Full Flight Simulators (FFS), Flight Training Devices (FTD), and others like Flight Simulation Systems (FBS) or Flight Management Systems (FMS). FFS are highly advanced simulators that replicate the complete flying experience, featuring realistic motion and visuals. FTDs provide essential training with limited motion capabilities, focusing on specific tasks. Other systems, such as FBS and FMS, enhance training through additional data analysis and scenarios, supporting various levels of pilot education.</p></p>
