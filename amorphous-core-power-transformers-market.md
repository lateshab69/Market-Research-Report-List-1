<p><p>The amorphous core power transformers market is divided into two main types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers use mineral oil for insulation and cooling, offering high efficiency and reliability, particularly in larger applications. In contrast, dry-type transformers utilize air or resin for insulation, making them safer and more environmentally friendly, ideal for indoor settings. Both types leverage amorphous steel cores to enhance efficiency, reduce losses, and improve overall performance compared to traditional silicon steel transformers.</p></p>
