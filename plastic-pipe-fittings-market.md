<p><p>The plastic pipe fittings market encompasses various materials, each with unique properties. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for its durability and chemical resistance, ideal for plumbing and drainage. CPVC (Chlorinated PVC) offers higher temperature tolerance, making it suitable for hot water applications. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is valued for its impact resistance and low-temperature performance. PE (Polyethylene) is flexible and resistant to corrosion, commonly used in gas and water supply systems. Nylon, known for strength and lightweight properties, is used in specialized applications.</p></p>
