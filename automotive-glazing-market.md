<p><p>The **Automotive Glazing Market** includes **Laminated Glass**, **Tempered Glass**, and **Polycarbonate** as key types. Laminated glass, made of two glass layers with a plastic interlayer, offers safety, UV protection, and noise reduction. Tempered glass, heat-treated for strength, is used in side and rear windows, shattering into small, safe pieces upon impact. Polycarbonate, a lightweight, impact-resistant plastic, is ideal for sunroofs and head-up displays, providing durability and design flexibility. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, balancing safety, performance, and aesthetics.</p></p>
