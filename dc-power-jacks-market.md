<p><p>The DC Power Jacks market is categorized into two primary types: Through Hole DC Power Jacks and Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks. Through Hole DC Power Jacks are designed for insertion into drilled holes on a printed circuit board (PCB), providing strong mechanical support and reliable connections, ideal for high-stress applications. In contrast, Surface Mounting DC Power Jacks are mounted directly onto the surface of the PCB, allowing for compact designs and facilitating automated assembly processes, which enhances manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
