<p><ul><li>Lens Stereoscope</li><li>Mirror Stereoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stereoscopic imaging system market includes various types, primarily lens stereoscopes and mirror stereoscopes. Lens stereoscopes utilize magnifying lenses to create depth perception by displaying two slightly offset images, enhancing viewing details. Conversely, mirror stereoscopes employ mirrors to reflect images, allowing for a more compact design while still achieving the 3D effect. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in photography, virtual reality, and medical imaging, appealing to consumers and professionals seeking immersive visual experiences and enhanced dimensionality.</p></p>
