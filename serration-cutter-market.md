<p><ul><li>Milling</li><li>Hob</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The serration cutter market primarily includes two main types: milling and hob cutters. Milling cutters are designed for high-precision machining operations, utilizing serrated teeth to achieve smooth surfaces and intricate shapes in various materials. In contrast, hob cutters focus on producing gears and other tooth profiles, employing a continuous cutting action that enhances efficiency. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs, catering to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and tool production, where precision and quality are crucial.</p></p>
