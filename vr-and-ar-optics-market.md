<p><strong>The VR and AR Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waveguide</li><li>Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VR and AR optics market comprises primarily two types: waveguide and lens-based systems. Waveguide optics utilize transparent materials to direct light from micro-displays directly to the user's eyes, enabling a lightweight design suitable for augmented reality applications. Conversely, lens-based optics employ traditional lenses to magnify and focus images, often found in virtual reality headsets. Both types serve distinct purposes, with waveguides favoring compactness and portability while lenses often provide higher image fidelity and depth perception.</p></p>
