<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Power Electronics market encompasses key components essential for efficient EV operation. The **Battery Management System (BMS)** monitors and optimizes battery performance and safety. The **On-Board Charger** facilitates AC-to-DC conversion for battery charging. The **Inverter** converts DC power from the battery to AC for the motor. The **Vehicle Control Unit (VCU)** or **Hybrid Control Unit (HCU)** manages powertrain and energy flow. The **Pedestrian Detection System** enhances safety using sensors. **Others** include auxiliary systems like DC-DC converters and thermal management, ensuring overall NEV functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
