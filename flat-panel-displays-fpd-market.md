<p><p>Flat Panel Displays (FPD) encompass several technologies, each with unique features. Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) displays are known for vibrant colors and high contrast, as each pixel emits its own light. Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD) use liquid crystals manipulated by electric fields, requiring a backlight, making them energy-efficient with good image quality. Plasma Display Panels (PDP) consist of electrically charged gases that produce light, offering excellent color reproduction and viewing angles, often used in large screens before declining in popularity.</p></p>
