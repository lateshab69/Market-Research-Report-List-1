<p><p>The plastics for household appliances market includes various types, each suited for specific applications. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) offers strength and gloss, ideal for casings. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is durable and cost-effective, commonly used in cables and pipes. POM (Polyoxymethylene) is known for its low friction and wear resistance, perfect for mechanical parts. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) is used for electrical components due to its thermal stability. Other materials may include alternatives like polycarbonate or nylon, catering to diverse appliance needs.</p></p>
