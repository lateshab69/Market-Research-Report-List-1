<p><p>The spinal implants materials market comprises several key types, including fusion, fixation, vertebral compression fracture (VCF) solutions, decompression, and motion preservation technologies. Fusion implants promote stability by joining vertebrae, while fixation devices enhance support during healing. VCF solutions address fractures, restoring vertebral height and alignment. Decompression implants relieve pressure on spinal nerves, improving patient comfort. Motion preservation implants aim to maintain natural movement in the spine, reducing dependency on more rigid fixations and improving long-term outcomes for patients.</p></p>
