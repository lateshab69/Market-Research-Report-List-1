<p><strong>The Airfreight Forwarding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Charter</li><li>Partial Charter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airfreight forwarding market can be categorized into two main types: full charter and partial charter. A full charter involves leasing an entire aircraft for transporting goods, offering dedicated capacity and control over schedules, making it ideal for large shipments or specialized cargo. In contrast, a partial charter allows multiple customers to share the capacity of a single aircraft, providing flexibility and cost-efficiency for smaller shipments. Both options cater to varying logistical needs and budgets in air freight transportation.</p></p>
