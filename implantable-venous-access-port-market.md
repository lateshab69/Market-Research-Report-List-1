<p><p>The implantable venous access port market is categorized primarily into titanium infusion ports and plastic infusion ports. Titanium infusion ports are known for their durability and biocompatibility, making them suitable for long-term use and minimizing the risk of infection. In contrast, plastic infusion ports are lighter and often more cost-effective, appealing to patients who require temporary access. Both types are designed to provide reliable venous access for patients undergoing long-term treatments, such as chemotherapy or parenteral nutrition.</p></p>
