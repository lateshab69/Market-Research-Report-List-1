<p><p>The industrial-use nitrile gloves market is categorized into two primary types: powder-free nitrile gloves and powdered nitrile gloves. Powder-free nitrile gloves are designed for users who prefer a latex-free option without any powdered substance that might cause skin irritation or contamination. They offer excellent tactile sensitivity and barrier protection. In contrast, powdered nitrile gloves contain a powder that aids in easy donning and removal, making them suitable for environments where quick glove changes are necessary, although they may pose a risk of contamination.</p></p>
