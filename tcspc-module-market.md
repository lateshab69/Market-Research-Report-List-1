<p><p>Time-Correlated Single Photon Counting (TCSPC) modules are classified into single-channel and multi-channel markets based on their operational capabilities. Single-channel modules focus on capturing and analyzing one photon stream at a time, making them ideal for simpler experiments or applications requiring lower complexity. In contrast, multi-channel modules can simultaneously process multiple photon sources, allowing for more complex experiments, enhanced data collection, and improved temporal resolution. This versatility and performance differentiation serves distinct research and industrial application needs.</p></p>
