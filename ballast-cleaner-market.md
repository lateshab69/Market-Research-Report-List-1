<p><strong>The Ballast Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shoulder Cleaner</li><li>Plain Track Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ballast cleaner market is segmented into two main types: shoulder cleaners and plain track cleaners. Shoulder cleaners are designed to maintain the ballast on the sides of the track, ensuring proper drainage and stability. They effectively remove debris and vegetation, enhancing track safety and performance. Plain track cleaners, on the other hand, target the ballast directly underneath the rails, removing accumulated dirt and improving ballast quality. Both types play a crucial role in railway maintenance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
