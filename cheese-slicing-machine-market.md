<p><p>The cheese slicing machine market is segmented into continuous and intermittent types. Continuous type slicing machines operate in a steady flow, ideal for high-volume production, ensuring consistent slice thickness and efficiency. In contrast, intermittent type slicing machines are designed for flexibility, allowing operators to slice cheese in batches or on-demand, suited for smaller productions or specialty cheeses. Both types cater to different industry needs, with continuous machines focusing on bulk processing, while intermittent machines prioritize versatility and customization.</p></p>
