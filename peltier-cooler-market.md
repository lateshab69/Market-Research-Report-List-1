<p><p>The Peltier cooler market comprises two main types: single-stage and multi-stage modules. Single-stage modules are designed for straightforward cooling applications, utilizing a single thermoelectric element to create a temperature differential. These are typically cost-effective and compact, ideal for small devices. In contrast, multi-stage modules consist of multiple thermoelectric elements stacked to achieve lower temperatures, making them suitable for more demanding applications like industrial cooling or medical devices. Their complexity allows for increased efficiency in temperature control across various sectors.</p></p>
