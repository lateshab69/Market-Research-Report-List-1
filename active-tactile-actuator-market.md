<p><strong>The Active Tactile Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The active tactile actuator market comprises various technologies designed to enhance haptic feedback in devices. Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators use an unbalanced motor to create vibrations, generating a tactile sensation often found in smartphones. Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA) produce vibrations through a mass oscillating at specific frequencies, offering more precise feedback for applications like gaming and wearables. Other types include piezoelectric and electroactive polymers, which provide unique feedback mechanisms tailored to specialized applications or industries.</p></p>
