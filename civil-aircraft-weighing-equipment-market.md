<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform System</li><li>Jack Weigh System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment market features two primary types: platform systems and jack weigh systems. </p><p>Platform systems involve a flat surface where the aircraft is positioned, measuring weight through integrated scales. These systems provide a stable and accurate method for assessing aircraft weight, ensuring compliance with safety regulations.</p><p>Jack weigh systems, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic or mechanical jacks to lift the aircraft, allowing for weight measurement at different points. This method offers flexibility in accessing various aircraft types and configurations.</p></p>
