<p><p>Freestanding Emergency Departments (FEDs) are categorized into two main types: Off-Campus Emergency Departments (OCED) and Independent Freestanding Emergency Departments (IFSED). OCEDs are affiliated with a hospital and typically provide emergency care in a location separate from the main hospital, enhancing access for patients. In contrast, IFSEDs operate independently, not tied to a particular hospital, offering emergency services with greater flexibility in operations and often focusing on convenience for patients without hospital affiliation. Both aim to meet urgent healthcare needs efficiently.</p></p>
