<p><p>The electrophoresis instrumentation market encompasses various types, including blotting instruments, vertical gel electrophoresis, and horizontal gel electrophoresis. Blotting instruments are used for transferring proteins or nucleic acids from gels to membranes for further analysis. Vertical gel electrophoresis is ideal for separating proteins based on size, commonly utilized in research settings. In contrast, horizontal gel electrophoresis is primarily used for nucleic acid separation and is popular in routine laboratories, offering ease of use and versatility for various applications.</p></p>
