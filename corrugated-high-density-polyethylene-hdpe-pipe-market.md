<p><p>The Corrugated High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Pipe market includes two primary types: Single-Wall and Double-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipes. Single-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipes are characterized by a lightweight structure, designed for gravity-flow applications, primarily in drainage and stormwater management. In contrast, Double-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipes feature an inner and outer wall, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for both gravity and pressure applications in sewer systems, industrial processes, and agricultural irrigation.</p></p>
