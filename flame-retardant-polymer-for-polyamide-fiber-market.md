<p><p>The flame retardant polymer market for polyamide fibers includes two primary approaches: blending methods and after-finishing techniques. The blending method incorporates flame retardant additives directly into the polymer matrix during the manufacturing process, resulting in inherent flame resistance. In contrast, after-finishing involves applying flame retardant treatments to the finished polyamide products, enhancing their fire resistance properties. Both methodologies aim to improve the safety and durability of polyamide fibers in various applications, particularly in textiles and engineering materials.</p></p>
