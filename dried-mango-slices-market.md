<p><p>The dried mango slices market encompasses several drying methods, primarily freeze-drying and vacuum drying. Vacuum drying involves removing moisture under reduced pressure, preserving color and taste while creating a chewy texture. Other methods may include air drying and solar drying, offering varying quality and shelf-life. Each technique influences the product's nutritional value, taste, and market appeal, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
