<p><p>The prepreg market consists of three main types: glass fiber prepreg, carbon fiber prepreg, and aramid fiber prepreg. Glass fiber prepreg is known for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, making it ideal for applications in construction and automotive sectors. Carbon fiber prepreg offers high strength-to-weight ratios, suited for aerospace and high-performance sports equipment. Aramid fiber prepreg is characterized by its resistance to abrasion and impact, often used in military and protective gear, providing both durability and lightweight properties. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
